About Me

Hello, my name is Justin Million. I am 23 years old and currently live in Kalispell, MT. I moved to Montana from St. Louis, MO in 2017. I love the outdoors and have many hobbies and interests including hiking, fishing, disc golfing, playing video games, kickboxing and more!


I currently work as an Associate Producer at KGEZ Radio in Kalispell, I also have a Real Estate License in Montana. As of late I have begun learning coding/web development. I’m new to it but I am really enjoying it and excited to see where it takes me.

As I love the outdoors and all the beauty Montana has to offer, I don’t foresee myself living here forever. I understand the importance of being flexible and am always open for an opportunity to gain more experience in different parts of the country.
